# CRM Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
A multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing CRM sales performance across regions, industries, and sales agents — covering revenue trends, sales pipeline health, forecasting, and lost opportunity analysis.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Provide sales leadership with a 360° view of pipeline performance, identify top-performing agents and industries, flag pipeline bottlenecks, and forecast future income.

## 🗂️ Data Source
CRM sales/deals dataset (February 2025 – March 2025 transaction period, deal-level data)

## 🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI, DAX, Power Query

## 🔍 Key Analysis
**Page 1 — CRM Analysis Report (Overview)**
- Revenue trends over time, conversion rates by product (SAAS, Services, Custom Solutions)
- Sales performance by industry and top-performing sales agents
- Geographic view of conversion efficiency across European markets

**Page 2 — Pipeline Health**
- Sales funnel from Opportunity → Customer, tracking drop-off at each stage
- Response time impact on conversion rate
- Sales cycle duration segmented by organization size
- Lost opportunities breakdown by industry and country

**Page 3 — Forecast & Accuracy**
- Forecasted income by sales agent and industry
- Actual vs expected close date tracking
- Future income forecasting model with confidence interval

## 💡 Key Insights
- Total revenue of $8.28M across 348 closed deals, with an average deal value of $2.76K
- Overall conversion rate of 5.70%, with SAAS products converting highest (5.79%)
- Laura Thompson led all sales agents with $2.05M in closed deals
- Average sales cycle duration was 63.17 days, with Energy & Utilities taking longest (81.5 days) and Education & Science fastest (48.2 days)
- $505,882 in total lost opportunity value identified across European markets, concentrated in Italy and the Netherlands
- Forecasted income of 3.71M with 0.99 forecast accuracy, though near-term trend signals a projected decline
